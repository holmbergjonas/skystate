# The Manifesto: Where we belong
I always dream big. I usually build small.

Even the smallest service needs memory. A place to call home.
And the moment I reach for storage, I lose focus. I lose momentum.

I leave my code and step into the world of hosting, migrations, and pipelines.

Building turns into babysitting.
Vision turns into worries.

I want things to be simple, from the start.
Safe, while ideas are fragile/tender.
Easy to migrate, when the time is right.

So I’m making infrastructure invisible,
so we can stay in the editor.

Where we belong.
